About Asian Hornets

Why They're Dangerous

A single Asian hornet can kill up to 50 honeybees per day. A small nest of 30 hornets can devastate a hive in hours.

How to Identify

Dark brown/black body, yellow-orange face, yellow legs. Smaller than European hornets — about 2–3 cm long.

Tracking the Spread

First detected in France in 2004, Asian hornets now cover most of Europe. Community reports are key to tracking expansion.
